How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide customer service

  • Send service notifications

  • Respond to inquiries

Information Sharing

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

All other categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties.

Information may be shared with subcontractors or service providers strictly for business operations such as customer support or SMS delivery.

SMS Messaging Terms & Conditions

By opting in to receive SMS messages from Superior Water Systems/Lee’s Water Systems, you agree to receive customer care and promotional text messages.

Types of Messages You May Receive

  • Service confirmations
  • Appointment reminders
  • Service updates
  • Customer support responses
  • Promotional offers

Message Frequency

Message frequency may vary.

Message & Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier plan.

Opt-Out Instructions

You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message.

After you send STOP, you will receive one confirmation message and will no longer receive SMS messages unless you opt back in by replying START.

How Users Provide Consent

Users may opt in to receive SMS messages by:

  • Submitting the contact form at contact and checking the SMS consent checkbox.
  • OR by texting START to our business number.
